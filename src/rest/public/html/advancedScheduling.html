No more sprinting across campus! This feature creates a location-optimized schedule based on user-inputted groups of courses. Example: CPSC 121 and CPSC 210 are often taken during the same term. If their sections are scheduled consecutively they will be scheduled in locations that are less than 8 minutes walk from each other in order to enable students to get from one class to the other during the 10 minute break between classes.
